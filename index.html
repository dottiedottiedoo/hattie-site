Xbox games

Celeste: 8/10

About: A 2D platform game where a young girl is climbing a mountain.

Good: Fast and simple (but tricky!). It has multiple levels of different scenary.

Bad: The retro graphics may not be your thing.

King's Quest: 9/10

About: Adventure and puzzle solving and a quest to be king.

Good: Funny characters and interesting problems to solve.

Bad: If you go back to some old places it sometimes takes a while to get back because he only jogs.

Lumo: 8/10

About: A girl trapped in a computer! She must get out - one 3D room at a time.

Good: Pretty to look at, some brilliant rooms and different surfaces on the floors: ice, stone and carpet.

Bad: At first, it is hard to find the computer in which you get stuck.

Trove: 7/10

About: A "massively multiplayer online role-playing game" but can also be played individually. You can mine or steal materials and use them to create different objects and rides.

Good: It has multiple worlds of different scenary. And has daily challenges to complete.

Bad: You need certain amounts of Trove Mastery to travel through some portals.

Sparkle 2: 8/10

About: Shoot balls, chains of the same colour of length three or more explode.

Good: Great power-ups.

Bad: Quite short.

Rime: 9/10

About: A young boy in a an imginary world made up by his father. A mix of puzzle and action.

Good: Great scenery and characters.

Bad: The answers to the puzzles can be quite puzzling.

Grabbed By The Ghoulies: 8/10

About:A boy(cooper) and his girlfreind/sister get trapped in a old haunted house owned by a mad proffesor and they must fight skeltons, zombies, the reaper and other monstorsto escape.

Good: Funny characters. Has extra challenges to unlock

Bad: It may feel a bit old
